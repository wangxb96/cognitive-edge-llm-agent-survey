# Taxonomy A: Foundations | 分类A：基础概念

## A1. Edge Computing and Edge Intelligence | 边缘计算与边缘智能

- EN: Device-edge-cloud hierarchy, data proximity, low-latency and local autonomy.
- 中文: 端-边-云分层、数据近源处理、低时延与本地自治。

### References

- Shi et al. Edge Computing: Vision and Challenges. IEEE IoT Journal, 2016.
- Zhou et al. Edge Intelligence: Paving the Last Mile of AI with Edge Computing. Proceedings of the IEEE, 2019.
- Deng et al. Edge intelligence: The confluence of edge computing and AI. IEEE IoT Journal, 2020.
- Li et al. Edge AI: On-demand accelerating DNN inference via edge computing. IEEE TWC, 2019.

## A2. LLM Foundations for Edge | 面向边缘的大模型基础

- 中文: Transformer 架构、规模化规律与能力涌现。

### References

- Brown et al. Language Models are Few-Shot Learners. NeurIPS, 2020.
