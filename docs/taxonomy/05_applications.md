# Taxonomy E: Applications | 分类E：应用场景

## E1. Mobility and Autonomous Systems | 移动与自动系统

- EN: Autonomous driving, vehicle-edge collaboration, real-time decision support.
- 中文: 自动驾驶、车端边缘协同与实时决策支持。

### References

- Zhou et al. Edge Intelligence. Proceedings of the IEEE, 2019.
- Deng et al. Edge intelligence: The confluence of edge computing and AI. IEEE IoT Journal, 2020.

## E2. Healthcare and Privacy-Sensitive Domains | 医疗与隐私敏感领域

- EN: Privacy-preserving personalization and secure model adaptation.
- 中文: 隐私保护约束下的个性化与安全模型适配。

### References

- Ali et al. Federated Learning for Privacy Preservation in Smart Healthcare Systems. IEEE JBHI, 2022.
- Dwork and Roth. The Algorithmic Foundations of Differential Privacy. 2014.

## E3. On-Device Consumer and Industrial AI | 端侧消费与工业AI

- EN: Mobile assistants, industrial support, multilingual retrieval and local automation.
- 中文: 移动助手、工业场景支持、多语检索与本地自动化。

### References

- Abdin et al. Phi-4 Technical Report. 2024.
- Team Gemma et al. Gemma: Open Models Based on Gemini Research and Technology. 2024.
- Mehta et al. OpenELM. ICML ES-FM Workshop, 2024.
- Marone et al. mmBERT: A Modern Multilingual Encoder with Annealed Language Learning. 2025.
